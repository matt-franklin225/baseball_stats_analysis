Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Jeff Kent?

Jeff Kent is 56 years old.

When was Jeff Kent born?

Jeff Kent was born on March 7, 1968.

Where was Jeff Kent born?

Jeff Kent was born in Bellflower, CA .

How tall is Jeff Kent?

Jeff Kent is 6-1 (185 cm) tall.

How much did Jeff Kent weigh when playing?

Jeff Kent weighed 185 lbs (83 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Jeff Kent play?

Jeff Kent played 17 seasons.

Is Jeff Kent in the Hall of Fame?

Jeff Kent has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Jeff Kent drafted?

Jeff Kent was drafted by the Toronto Blue Jays in the 20th round of the 1989 MLB June Amateur Draft from University of California, Berkeley (Berkeley, CA) .

What position did Jeff Kent play?

Jeff Kent was a Second Baseman, Third Baseman and First Baseman.

How many hits did Jeff Kent have?

Jeff Kent had 2,461 hits over his career.

How many home runs did Jeff Kent have?

Jeff Kent had 377 home runs over his career.

What was Jeff Kent's average?

Jeff Kent had a .290 average over his career.

How many teams has Jeff Kent played for?

Jeff Kent played for 6 teams; the New York Mets, Toronto Blue Jays, Cleveland Indians, San Francisco Giants, Houston Astros and Los Angeles Dodgers.

How many World Series has Jeff Kent won?

Jeff Kent has never won a World Series.

When did Jeff Kent retire?

Jeff Kent last played in 2008.
