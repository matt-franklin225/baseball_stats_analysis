Barry Bonds

Position: Leftfielder

Bats: Left  • Throws: Left

6-1 , 185lb (185cm, 83kg)

Born: July 24 , 1964 in Riverside, CA us

Draft : Drafted by the San Francisco Giants in the 2nd round of the 1982 MLB June Amateur Draft from Junipero Serra HS (San Mateo, CA) and the Pittsburgh Pirates in the 1st round (6th) of the 1985 MLB June Amateur Draft from Arizona State University (Tempe, AZ) .

High School: Junipero Serra HS (San Mateo, CA)

School: Arizona State University (Tempe, AZ)

Debut: May 30, 1986 (Age 21-310d, 15,165th in major league history)
vs. LAD 5 AB, 0 H, 0 HR, 0 RBI, 0 SB

Last Game: September 26, 2007 (Age 43-064d)
vs. SDP 3 AB, 0 H, 0 HR, 0 RBI, 0 SB

Rookie Status: Exceeded rookie limits during 1986 season

Agents : Jeff Borris • Previously : Dennis Gilbert, Rod Wright, Scott Boras

Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Barry Bonds?

Barry Bonds is 59 years old.

When was Barry Bonds born?

Barry Bonds was born on July 24, 1964.

Where was Barry Bonds born?

Barry Bonds was born in Riverside, CA .

How tall is Barry Bonds?

Barry Bonds is 6-1 (185 cm) tall.

How much did Barry Bonds weigh when playing?

Barry Bonds weighed 185 lbs (83 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Barry Bonds play?

Barry Bonds played 22 seasons.

Is Barry Bonds in the Hall of Fame?

Barry Bonds has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Barry Bonds drafted?

Barry Bonds was drafted by the San Francisco Giants in the 2nd round of the 1982 MLB June Amateur Draft from Junipero Serra HS (San Mateo, CA) and the Pittsburgh Pirates in the 1st round (6th) of the 1985 MLB June Amateur Draft from Arizona State University (Tempe, AZ) .

What position did Barry Bonds play?

Barry Bonds was a Leftfielder.

How many hits did Barry Bonds have?

Barry Bonds had 2,935 hits over his career.

How many home runs did Barry Bonds have?

Barry Bonds had 762 home runs over his career.

What was Barry Bonds' average?

Barry Bonds had a .298 average over his career.

How many teams has Barry Bonds played for?

Barry Bonds played for 2 teams; the Pittsburgh Pirates and San Francisco Giants.

How many World Series has Barry Bonds won?

Barry Bonds has never won a World Series.

When did Barry Bonds retire?

Barry Bonds last played in 2007.

What is Barry Bonds' Twitter account?

Barry Bonds is on Twitter at BarryBonds .
